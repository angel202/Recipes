Lasagna Recipe



Freshly baked lasagna

This beautiful lasagna can be prepared in under 30 minutes! Yes, 30 minutes from prep time to cooking time. Follow along to see what ingridients you need, as well, what the steps are to making this lasagna.

Ingridients you will need

  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Ground beef
  • Ground Pork
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Basil

Steps to make lasagna

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Cook and brown ground beef and pork
  3. Mix pasta sauce with meat
  4. Start layering the pasta and alternate with meat and cheese
