Easy Bake Cheesecake

Easy bake cheesecake that can be done in 30 minutes.
This recipe is quick and easy. It only requires a few ingridients!

Ingridients

  • Cream cheese
  • Condensed milk
  • Sweetner of choice
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Graham Cookies

Steps to make cheesecake

  1. Mix all wet ingridients
  2. Crush graham crackers and mix with butter
  3. Bake graham cracker crust before poruing in mixture
  4. Once graham crust is baked, pour cheesecake mix in
  5. Bake for 30 minutes
  6. Once baked let cool in fridge for 2-4 hours and serve
