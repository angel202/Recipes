Simple Beans



Beans!

This is a simple recipe you can prep ahead of time
All you need is some dried beans, salt and water

Ingridients

  • Beans
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Seasonings of choice

Steps for making the beans

  1. In a large pot pour beans and cover with water
  2. Let beans soak for a few hours or overnight
  3. After lettings beans soak bring to a boil and then reduce heat to let simmer
  4. Simmer beans for two to four hours or until soft and tender
  5. Season with salt and seasonings of choice
